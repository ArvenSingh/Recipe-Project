Ramen Recipe

Description

Ramen is a Japanese noodle dish. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a broth; common flavors are soy sauce and miso, with typical toppings including sliced pork, nori (dried seaweed), menma (bamboo shoots), and scallions.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles.
  • Garlic and Ginger.
  • Dried shittake mushrooms.
  • Veggies like carrots or kale.
  • All of your favorite toppings like some panko, egg, chilli, etc.

Steps

  1. Stir fry the aromatics (garlic and ginger).
  2. Add some chicken broth and dried shittake mushrooms for some umami punch.
  3. Add the ramen noodles, cook them right in the broth with some scallions.
  4. Add veggies: thinly sliced kale, shredded carrots, whatever you'd like! cook until just tender.
  5. Top it off with some crunchy panko crumbs, a soft-boiled egg, chilli oil, hot sauce, sesame oil, and/or soy sauce, whatever your heart desires.
