Boiled Egg Recipe

Description

Boiled eggs are eggs, typically from a chicken, cooked with their shells unbroken, usually by immersion in boiling water.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Steaming Pot with Lid
  • Water

Steps

  1. Fill the steaming pot with some water.
  2. Put the steaming pot on the stove at medium heat.
  3. Put the eggs in the steaming pot.
  4. Put the lid on (if you prefer, it's not required).
  5. Turn the stove off after 10 minutes.
  6. Take the eggs out and peel off the egg's shell.
